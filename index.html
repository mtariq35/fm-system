🚀 How to go live in 3 steps
1
Host the files — Go to github.com, drag all 5 HTML files in. You'll get a free live URL instantly (e.g. https://mtariq35.github.io/fm-system)
2
Update QR codes — Open the QR Generator, paste your new live URL, select all locations, regenerate and print all posters
3
Post & share — Laminate and post QR posters in each location. Share the supervisor dashboard link with your 3 supervisors and the analytics link for yourself
